Jazz

Jazz is a improvisational music genre orginating from African-American communities.
Ever since the 1920's, Jazz has been recognized for its musical form of expression in tradtional music but also today's music.

Classical

Classical music is tradtional music that is known for being composed for performances by orchestras. It more generally refers to music from the Western Hempishere, aging back to the Romantic Era.

Rock

Emerging from the 1950s,rock music combines R&B, country, and jazz with a combination of different instruments. It is a popular music genre, associated with rhythms.
